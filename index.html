Our Story

Two Cultures,
One Table

Born from the vision of two chefs who believe food is the most honest form of storytelling, HANJI found its home in the heart of Washington, D.C. — where the precision of Korean culinary tradition meets the bold confidence of American gastronomy.

Every plate is a dialogue between two worlds: fermented and fresh, delicate and robust, ancient and modern. We source the finest regional ingredients and pair them with house-made Korean condiments, aged spirits from both hemispheres, and a commitment to craft that borders on obsession.

Ji-Yeon Park Executive Chef & Co-Founder
James Morrison Head Chef & Co-Founder
